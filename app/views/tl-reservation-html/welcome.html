Reserve a Testline

Don't know how to reserve for a testline?
On your team's page, just click "Use Now" or "Reserve Now" on the testlines that are available or in-use

Who's in queue?

Want to keep track of who's in line?
Click the reservations button on the navbar and select the testline you want to know who's in line for.

Release a Testline

To release a testline you're using or cancel a reservation, click the reservations button in the navbar, select the testline, and click release.
